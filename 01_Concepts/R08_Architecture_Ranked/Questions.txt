Senior iOS Interview Questions — Architecture & Design Patterns — Ranked by Probability

Each question has an explicit % likelihood of being asked in a
senior/staff-level architecture-focused interview.
Sorted strictly from most to least likely. Focus prep top-down.


99% — MVVM in SwiftUI

How do you implement MVVM in SwiftUI? Where does business logic live
and how do you define ViewModel boundaries?
Follow-up: How do you avoid the "massive ViewModel" problem?
Follow-up: How do you unit test a SwiftUI ViewModel?


98% — Dependency injection
