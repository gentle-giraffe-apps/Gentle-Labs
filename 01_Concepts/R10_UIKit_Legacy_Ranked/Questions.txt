Senior iOS Interview Questions — UIKit & Legacy — Ranked by Probability

Each question has an explicit % likelihood of being asked in a
senior/staff-level interview at a company with a mature iOS codebase.
Sorted strictly from most to least likely. Focus prep top-down.
